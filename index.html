Process
How It Works
01
Submit a Project

Community members submit small, practical projects that could benefit from volunteer support and shared labor. No project too scrappy.

02
Review + Organize

Projects are reviewed for fit, safety, and whether they make sense for a hands-on community build day. We make a plan.

03
Build Together

Volunteers show up, learn something, meet people, and help complete useful work that actually improves the city. That's it.

Scope
What We Build
Type 01
Cleanup + Prep

Clearing spaces, removing debris, organizing materials, preparing areas for improvement. The unglamorous work that makes everything else possible.

Type 02
Painting + Refresh

Simple visual improvements that help spaces feel more cared for, functional, and welcoming. Big visible impact for real effort.

Type 03
Yard + Exterior

Outdoor cleanup, curb appeal improvements, straightforward exterior work. Stuff that makes a block look like people give a damn.

Type 04
Beginner Repairs

Simple, lower-risk tasks that make sense for shared learning. Good for first-timers who want to try something with their hands.

Why We Exist
Baltimore has real work to do. That work creates opportunities for practical contribution. Practical contribution creates opportunities for shared effort. Shared effort creates community. Community, combined with hands-on work, creates learning. Learning builds confidence and visible value. And when the work is shared and meaningful, it can also be fun.
